Dota2 BuildSimulator

Introduction

Dota2 BuildSimulator allows you to create and edit your custom builds for each heroes, see heroes details (characteristics, abilities, talent tree, roles, …) and see how heroes evolve according to their levels, and items details (bonuses, receipts, cost, …)

All Dota 2 heroes are available with skills descriptions and talent tree.
All Dota 2 items are available with additional information for each main attribute.
Hero characteristics are automatically updated with items, hero level, abilities and talents.

Create build

Select a hero

Hero level starts at 1, increase it to desired level by clicking on Level Up button.
    

Items

For add items, click on empty placeholder, on Build Items inventory and select your items (items are added as 'Lock Combining').
Bonuses items are immediately added to the characteristics of the hero. Power Treads: you must choose Attribute.
Boots of Travels, Dagon and Necronomicon: you must choose item Level.
Bloodstone: mana regen is calculated with 14 charges.
        

Click on image item and select info icon for open item detail or select trash icon for remove it.
        

Items inventory for different game stages (starting, early and mid) are available.

Skills

Upgrade hero abilities for each level.
        

For choose talent, click on cell when those is available, and select branch for the level.
        

Reset

Reset Skills: Reset abilities and talents points. Keep selected items and hero level.
Reset All: Reset abilities and talents points, clear all selected items, and hero level back to 1.

Auras

Only bonuses of the permanent auras of items and abilities are added to hero stats and displayed.
Aura buffs: increase for allied units.
Aura debuffs: reduction for enemies units.

Note

You can enter a note for your build (optional).

Role

Click on Role button and select a role for your build (optional).

Save

Click on disk icon, enter a name for your build and click on save.
