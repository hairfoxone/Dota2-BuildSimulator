Dota2 BuildSimulator

Introduction

Dota2 BuildSimulator allows you to create and edit your custom builds for each heroes, see heroes details (characteristics, abilities, talent tree, roles, …) and see how heroes evolve according to their levels, and items details (bonuses, receipts, cost, …)

All Dota 2 heroes are available with skills descriptions and talent tree.
All Dota 2 items are available with additional information for each main attribute.
Hero characteristics are automatically updated with items, hero level, abilities and talents.
